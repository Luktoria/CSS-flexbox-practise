Meet the team

Milano Coffee is located in the tiny village, Felsőzsebalja. We just call it Milano to make it sound more classy.

Located conveniently just down the train-station, Milano Coffee stands unique in our village. Being the only coffe in Felsőzsebalja, we don't have to be affraid of competition (too much honesty might just end our business though).

Local green tea

First of all, our coffee is proud to present its best product, the tea. 100% homegrown leaves, locally sourced.

Local cookies

Our selection of tea cakes, biscuits and cookies are made by our lovely (and very bored) grandmothers. The products change day by day, depending on what the grannies feel like baking that day.

Local coffee
